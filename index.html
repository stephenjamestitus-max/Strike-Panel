Everything you need

What if I lose the file or get a new phone? +
Re-download anytime from your Gumroad receipt email — it lives there permanently. On a new device, open the file and install it as a PWA in under 10 seconds. Your data syncs across devices if you add a free Supabase cloud backup in Settings.
How does the AI setup work? Is it complicated? +
Go to aistudio.google.com, sign in with Google, click "Create API Key" — takes about 2 minutes, no credit card. Paste the key into Settings inside StrikePanel once and both AI Workout Generator and Food AI activate immediately. No ongoing cost.
Does it work on iPhone and Android? +
Yes — StrikePanel is a PWA (Progressive Web App) that runs in any browser. Open it in Safari on iPhone or Chrome on Android, tap "Add to Home Screen", and it installs like a native app. Works fully offline after first load.
Can my athletes use it on their own phones? +
Yes. The athlete self check-in feature generates a shareable link. Send it to your fighters — they open it on any phone, rate their sleep, energy, soreness and motivation in 30 seconds, and the scores appear in your Morning Brief automatically.
